National Strings Meeting, 2024

Department of Physics, IIT Ropar | 9th-14th December 2024

National Strings Meeting (NSM) is a biennial conference where the participants working in string theory, quantum field theory and related areas from Indian institutes/universities attend and present their works. NSM 2024 will be hosted by the Department of Physics, IIT Ropar from the 9th December to 14th December, 2024. The NSM is intended to foster interactions, stimulating discussions, exchange of ideas, and collaborations among young researchers and experts. The conference includes a few review talks, technical talks, poster presentations and focussed discussion sessions.
